    • About Uncle Iroh

    History

    Uncle Iroh, once a renowned general in the Fire Nation, was known for his military brilliance and strength. He earned the title "Dragon of the West" and was originally in line to become Fire Lord.

    Life Journey

    After the loss of his beloved son Lu Ten during the siege of Ba Sing Se, Iroh chose a new path: one of peace, wisdom, and compassion. He stepped away from war and became a mentor and father figure to his nephew, Prince Zuko.

    Hobbies

    Iroh finds joy in the simple things. He enjoys brewing and tasting tea, playing the tsungi horn, gardening, and stargazing. His favorite tea? Jasmine, of course.

    Interests

    Iroh has a deep appreciation for philosophy, balance, nature, and inner peace. He often shares poetic advice and reflects on the balance between the four elements - and within the human spirit.

    Inspiration

    The memory of his son greatly shaped Iroh's values. His compassion, humility, and wisdom inspired generations - not only within the Fire Nation, but far beyond it. His story reminds us all that true strength comes from kindness.
